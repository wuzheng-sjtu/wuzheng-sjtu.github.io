About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at University of California, Berkeley, working with Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka. I am also a part-time AI resident at X, the moonshot factory (formerly Google[x]). My current research interests lie at designing reward learning algorithms to make the robot better interact with the environment.

Before coming to UC Berkeley, I received my B.S. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019. During my undergraduate years, I was fortunate to work with Cewu Lu at SJTU, Jiajun Wu and Josh Tenenbaum at MIT.
